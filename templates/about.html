About Pancreatic Cancer Detection

What is Pancreatic Cancer?

Pancreatic cancer is a form of cancer that begins in the tissues of the pancreas. It's particularly challenging to detect early, making advanced imaging techniques crucial for diagnosis.

Types of Pancreatic Cancer
  • Ductal Adenocarcinoma

    The most common type, starting in the ducts of the pancreas

  • Acinar Cell Carcinoma

    Rare type starting in enzyme-producing cells

  • Neuroendocrine Tumors

    Affecting hormone-producing cells of the pancreas
